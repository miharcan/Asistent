The ASISTENT or assistant@en was developed to translate text between English, Slovene, Serbian and the Croatian language.

Additional to the ASISTENT web interface, a RESTful service is available, which enables the access of ASISTENT with other applications. For more information visit this website.

The Asistent translation System was presented at the JTDH'16 conferece in Ljubljana, Slovenia (link to the paper). If you use any parts of Asistent in your own work, please cite the following publication:
